# 🥤 CodeX Energy Drinks – Consumer Insights & Marketing Analysis

A data analytics project that analyzes **10,000 consumer survey responses** to help **CodeX Energy Drinks**, a German beverage company, understand the Indian market and improve its marketing strategy.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

CodeX is a German energy drink brand planning to strengthen its presence in India. Using SQL-based data analysis on survey responses, this project uncovers consumer preferences, buying behavior, marketing effectiveness, and product improvement opportunities.

The insights are visualized in a PowerPoint dashboard to support data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

The company wanted to answer questions such as:

- Who consumes energy drinks the most?
- Which age group is the primary target audience?
- Which marketing channels influence young consumers?
- What packaging do customers prefer?
- Who are the current market leaders?
- Which sales channels are most preferred?
- What pricing strategy should be adopted?
- What improvements should be made to the product?
- Which marketing campaigns can increase brand awareness?

- Limited-time offers

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- Young consumers (15–30 years) are the primary target audience.
- Social media is the most effective marketing channel.
- Mid-range pricing attracts the largest customer base.
- Attractive packaging significantly influences buying decisions.
- Consumers value taste, energy boost, and affordability.
- Retail stores and supermarkets remain popular purchase locations.
- Brand awareness can be improved through influencer marketing and fitness partnerships.
